What Is Dropshipping

Dropshipping is a business model where you, as a store owner, don’t need to buy products in advance. You just add a necessary product to your site and when someone purchases it, you buy it from supplier who ships this item directly to your customer.

Many aspiring entrepreneurs started their journey into e-commerce through dropshipping as it is the easiest and cheapest way to start. One of the most inspiring dropshipping success stories will be the story of Zappos, the dropshipping stores that dominates the US shoes market. Zappos currently stocks 3 million pairs of shoes, handbags, apparel and accessories. More than 1,000 brands which you can’t easily find in other offline store. And they were purchased by Amazon in 2009 for $1.2 billion! It all started when they decided to base the company's business using the dropshipping model.

Here's other benefits of dropshipping
  • Low Startup Capital: You don't need to buy any product in advance. Only pay the dropship supplier after the customer pays you
  • Wide Selection of Products: There will be a dropship supplier for almost any products (large and small)
  • Highly Scalable: Since most of the works are handled by the dropship supplier, your business can scale up without much extra work and effort
  • Lower Risk: Even if the business does not work out, you wont get stuck with a lot of product that can't sell (since you did not purchase any product in advance)
  • Sell Anywhere: You can practically sell any products anywhere in the world as long as your dropship supplier can ship to that location
